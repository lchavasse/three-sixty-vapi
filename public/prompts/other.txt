# Other Stakeholders

*Additional stakeholders who have had a close relationship with the subject.*

e.g. Mentor, Teacher, Customer, Parents!?

## Purpose

To gain an additional outside-in perspective on the subject.

## Example Questions

- What are their notable strengths or positive qualities in your interactions? (*For a client, this could be about responsiveness or expertise; for a mentor, it might be about character or willingness to learn.*)
- Have you observed any challenges or areas for improvement in how the subject operates?
- [if applicable] How have you seen the subject grow over the time you have known them? What explicit actions did they take to encourage this?
