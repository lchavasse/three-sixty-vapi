# Personal Feedback

*Someone who has a close relationship with the subject on a strictly personal level*

## Purpose

Gain depth of insight into the subject's psyche in order to understand the full context around their performance.

## Example Questions

- How does this individual build and maintain your trust?
- What would you say their greatest priority is in life right now?
- If there was one quality about the subject that you wish would never change, what would it be?
- In the last year, what has the subject done that helped you the most? What action have they taken that has most surprised you?
- What is one thing you admire about the subject?
