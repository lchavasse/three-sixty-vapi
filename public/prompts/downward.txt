# Downward Feedback

*A person in a direct managerial or supervisory position*

## Purpose

Insight on the subject's results, impact, and growth, praising performance and highlighting weaknesses.

## Example Questions

- What is an area where youâ€™ve seen this person excel?
- What are the strengths of the subject as a leader?
- Share an example of an achievement that highlights those strengths.
- What are the most significant challenges and/or opportunities facing the subject today?
- How do they help you now? How can they help you better going forward?
- If you were to give one piece of advice to help this leader grow, what would it be?
- How well does this person manage their workload?
- How effectively did this person communicate with others?
- How well did this person adapt to changing priorities?
